# Padma

Padma is a clinical pathway and care coordination application for managing patients through evidence-based care journeys.

It helps care teams enroll patients into structured pathways, coordinate tasks across roles, monitor progress, communicate with patients, and keep pathway decisions visible over time. Padma is designed for chronic care, rehabilitation, post-discharge follow-up, and other programs where patients move through defined stages of care.

## What Padma Supports

- Clinical pathways with stages such as assessment, treatment, monitoring, escalation, graduation, or discharge
- Care plans made up of interventions, tasks, reminders, and follow-ups
- Patient enrollment into the right pathway with the right care team
- Coordinator worklists for upcoming, overdue, assigned, and completed tasks
- Patient-level monitoring across pathway status, tasks, care-team notes, and history
- Internal care-team discussion around a patient or pathway enrollment
- Patient communication through approved templates and tracked message history
- Privacy consent capture and patient data request workflows
- Named care teams that can be reused across pathways and enrollments
- Pathway builder workflows for designing stages, interventions, and transition rules

## Care Settings

Padma is built for care that moves across settings:

- Outpatient care
- Inpatient care
- Home care

A patient can move from one setting to another as their care journey changes. Pathway stages, care tasks, communication, and monitoring can all reflect the patient’s current setting.

## Core Workflows

### Design a Pathway

Clinical and operations teams can define a pathway, add ordered stages, attach care tasks, and describe when a patient should move from one stage to another.

### Enroll a Patient

Care teams can select a patient, choose a pathway, assign the responsible team, and start the care journey.

### Coordinate Daily Care

Coordinators can track patient tasks, complete or reassign work, monitor adherence, identify overdue actions, and follow up with the care team or patient.

### Monitor a Patient Journey

Each patient has a workspace that brings together their pathway, current stage, task status, communication history, care-team discussion, and timeline.

### Communicate with Patients

Teams can use approved message templates, view inbound and outbound communication history, and keep patient outreach connected to the care journey.

### Manage Privacy and Consent

Padma includes workflows for recording consent, reviewing patient privacy status, and supporting patient data requests.

## Who Uses Padma

- Care coordinators managing daily pathway activity
- Nurses and physicians completing clinical tasks and reviewing progress
- Supervisors monitoring workload, overdue care, and program performance
- Administrators maintaining pathways, care teams, users, roles, and settings

## Current Focus

Padma currently covers the core clinical pathway and care coordination experience, including pathway design, patient enrollment, task management, patient monitoring, team communication, patient messaging, and privacy workflows.

The remaining product focus is deeper automation, stronger real-time updates, broader external-system connectivity, advanced patient segmentation, and production hardening.
